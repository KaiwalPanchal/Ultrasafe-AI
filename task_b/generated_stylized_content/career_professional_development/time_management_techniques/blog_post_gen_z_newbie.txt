## How to Actually Do It: Tips That Slap

### Pomodoro Technique

Work for 25 minutes straight (no TikTok, promise), then take a 5-minute breather. Repeat. Perfect for those long homework sessions or creative work.

### Eisenhower Matrix

Sort your tasks like this:

- Urgent & Important: Do it NOW.
- Important, Not Urgent: Schedule it.
- Urgent, Not Important: Ask someone else to do it.
- Not Urgent, Not Important: Trash it.

### Time Blocking

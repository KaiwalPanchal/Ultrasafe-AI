## Core Ideas to Keep in Mind

Let‚Äôs keep it simple with these basics:

- **Prioritize:** Tackle urgent and important stuff first.
- **Set Goals:** Clear, doable targets keep you on track.
- **Plan:** Use to-do lists or calendars to organize your day.

## Time Management in Action

Here are some popular tricks that really work:

- **Pomodoro Technique:** Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat! Great for keeping energy up.
- **Eisenhower Matrix:** Sort tasks by urgency and importance so you know what to do, schedule, delegate, or ditch.
- **Time Blocking:** Block chunks of your calendar for specific tasks ‚Äî Elon Musk and Bill Gates swear by this!
- **Getting Things Done (GTD):** Capture tasks outside your head, sort them, and act on what matters.
- **Apps:** Tools like Trello, RescueTime, and Todoist help you stay organized digitally.

*FYI:* A Harvard Business Review survey (2021) found that people who time block get **20% more done** and feel **15% less stressed**.

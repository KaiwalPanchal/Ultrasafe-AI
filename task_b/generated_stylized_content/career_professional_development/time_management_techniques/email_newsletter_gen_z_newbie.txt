### Core Moves to Try

### Real-Life Hacks You Can Use

- **Pomodoro:** Work 25 minutes, break 5 ‚Äî repeat. Perfect for staying alert and avoiding burnout.
- **Eisenhower Matrix:** Split tasks into four boxes:
  - Urgent & Important = do it NOW
  - Important, Not Urgent = schedule it
  - Urgent, Not Important = hand it off
  - Neither = ditch it
- **Time Blocking:** Block out chunks on your calendar for focused work (yes, even celebs like Elon Musk do this).
- **Getting Things Done (GTD):** Capture and organize tasks outside your head to stay cool and clear.

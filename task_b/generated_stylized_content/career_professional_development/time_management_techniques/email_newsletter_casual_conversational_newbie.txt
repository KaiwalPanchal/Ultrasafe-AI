### Real-Life Tips You Can Try Now

- **Pomodoro Technique:** Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat! Great for keeping your energy up.  
- **Eisenhower Matrix:** Sort your tasks like this:  
  1. Urgent & Important = Do now  
  2. Important, Not Urgent = Schedule it  
  3. Urgent, Not Important = Delegate it  
  4. Neither = Toss it out  
- **Time Blocking:** Block off chunks on your calendar for specific things you want to get done. Big bosses like Elon Musk swear by this!  
- **Getting Things Done (GTD):** Write everything down, organize it, and check things off. This method helps clear your mind.  
- **Apps:** Try tools like Trello, RescueTime, or Todoist for organizing and tracking your tasks digitally.  

**Quick heads-up:** A Harvard Business Review survey found that people who use time blocking got **20% more productive** and felt **15% less stressed**.

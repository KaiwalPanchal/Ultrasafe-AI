## Key Things to Know

### Transferable Skills  
These are your ‚Äúsuperpowers‚Äù that work across jobs ‚Äî think leadership, problem-solving, and even great communication. Spotting and using these skills helps you break into new industries way easier.

### Reskilling & Upskilling  
You might need to learn new skills (reskilling) or make your current ones even stronger (upskilling). This could mean taking classes, workshops, or even online tutorials.

### Networking & Mentorship  
Getting to know people in your new field opens doors and gives you advice. A mentor can be your guide through this new journey.

### Personal Branding  
Showing off your value in a way that clicks with your target industry helps shift any doubts about your experience.

### Research & Market Analysis  
Knowing the trends, needed qualifications, and what employers want gives you a solid advantage before you jump in.

  **Fact:** Pivots can be about growth, change, or aligning with your values.

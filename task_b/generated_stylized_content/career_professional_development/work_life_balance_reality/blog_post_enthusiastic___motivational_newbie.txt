## Easy-Peasy Boundary Basics You Can Start Today

### 1. What Are Boundaries?

Think of boundaries as invisible fences around your time and space ‚Äî they keep work from sneaking into your personal life. Simple examples:

- Not replying to work emails after a certain time.
- Having a special spot at home that's just for work tasks.

### 2. Time Management

Making a plan for your day helps so much! Use calendars or simple to-do lists to give yourself blocks of time for work, and just as much for your personal life.

### 3. Segmentation vs. Integration

- **Segmentation** means keeping work and home life totally separate.
- **Integration** blends bits of both into your day.

### 4. Psychological Detachment

This fancy phrase just means: ‚Äúswitch off‚Äù from work in your mind when your workday is done. It helps reduce stress and gives your brain a break so you can recharge.

Tech helps us stay connected, but it can also sneak in and mess with your free time. Use features like ‚ÄúDo Not Disturb,‚Äù set times to check emails, and try to avoid multitasking when you‚Äôre off the clock.

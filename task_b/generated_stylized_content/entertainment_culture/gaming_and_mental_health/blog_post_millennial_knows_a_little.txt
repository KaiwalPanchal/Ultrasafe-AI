## What Exactly Is Gaming Disorder?

Think of gaming disorder as where gaming stops being fun and starts taking over your life. The WHO defines it as:

- Not being able to control your gaming time  
- Prioritizing gaming over other parts of your life, like work or friends  
- Continuing to game even when it causes problems  

This pattern needs to last at least a year to be considered a disorder. So if you or someone you know fits this description, it might be time to get some help.

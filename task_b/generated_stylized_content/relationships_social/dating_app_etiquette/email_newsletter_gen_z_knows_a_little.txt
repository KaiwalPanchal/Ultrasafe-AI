### What’s Dating App Etiquette Anyway?

Think of it as the *unwritten* guide to behaving like a pro on dating apps. It covers everything from making a real-deal profile, how to chat without ghosting, to safely leveling up to that first date. Since apps like Tinder & Bumble boast over 370 million users worldwide (yeah, that’s wild), knowing these nods and no-nos is a total must.

### The Numbers That Matter 💡

- Over 30% of U.S. adults have used dating apps (Pew Research Center, 2023) — and *48%* of 18-29-year-olds are totally in the mix.

### Keep It Real: Core Etiquette Tips ✨

- **Be Your Authentic AF Self:** Honest pics + true-to-you bios = trust vibes.  
- **Respect the Chat:** Quick, polite replies show you care about their time.  
- **Consent is Key:** Always check boundaries before sharing pics or moving fast.  
- **Close the Loop:** If interest fades, say it kindly instead of disappearing.  
- **Know Your App’s Rules:** Bumble’s ladies-first vibes? Respect that!  
- **Guard Your Privacy:** Don’t overshare; keep your personal info on lock.  

---

### How This Plays Out IRL  

- **Profile Pro:** John posts clear pics — a headshot, hobby shot, and full body — plus a bio like “avid hiker ☀️, book lover 📚, coffee fan ☕.” Result? 60% higher chances of matches (OkCupid, 2021).  
- **Opening Line Win:** Sara messages first on Bumble with a “Hey, love your jazz taste! Any concert faves lately?” That little personalization ups reply chances by 30%.  
- **Ghosting Vibes:** Alex chats with Mia for a week, then… crickets. Instead of stressing, he respects the vibe and moves on — because ghosting’s sadly normal.  
- **Date Prep:** Double-check time, place, and comfort (public spots & a heads-up to a friend). Safety first, always.

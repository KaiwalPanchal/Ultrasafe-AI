🔥 1/8  
Alright fam, here’s the tea ☕️ on friendship maintenance as adults — it’s NOT just about chilling all day like in school. Adulting = busy lives, moves, jobs, babies… so keeping those bonds tight takes some real effort. But trust me, it’s worth it!

👯‍♀️ 2/8  
Did you know? 83% of adults say friendships are *key* for mental health (APA, 2019). But we usually roll with 3-5 close friends now — smaller crew, but way more meaningful (Roberts & Dunbar, 2011). Quality over quantity fr.

📞 3/8  
Here’s the deal: You don’t gotta chat every day to keep the friendship vibe alive. Meaningful convos and check-ins count big time (Rawlins, 1992). Even a quick “you good?” text lowkey saves connections.

🤝 4/8  
Friendship maintenance = give & take. No one-sided vibes allowed. Also, handling conflicts like a boss and forgiving keeps trust solid. Adult social circles shrink but become way more emotionally lit. Prioritize your main squad.

🚀 5/8  
Life changes mess with friendships a lot — new jobs, moving, babies poppin’ up. But scheduling video calls, planning hangouts, or making group activities (like hiking clubs) can keep that flame alive. Tech is your bestie here.

💡 6/8  
Not gonna lie, some myths gotta go:  
- Friendships don’t last *just* cuz — you gotta nurture ’em.  
- No daily contact ≠ weak friendship.  
- Different friendships serve diff vibes — some deep, some chill.  
- Distance? Nah, it’s all about effort, not miles.

🧠 7/8  
Expert spill: Dr. Rawlins says it’s about responsiveness, not constant presence. Dr. Holt-Lunstad reminds us friendships boost health like, majorly — 50% higher survival odds, less stress, better immune function (Holt-Lunstad, 2010). Big mood, right?

✅ 8/8  
So, wanna glow up your adult friendships? Try this:  
- Schedule regular meetups/ calls 📅  
- Keep convos real & meaningful  
- Use tech to stay close 💻  
- Be there for your friends, even lowkey  
- Handle beef calmly  
- Build fun rituals (monthly dinners, annual trips)  
Got tips? Spill the tea 👇 RT if you learned! #FriendshipGoals #Adulting

## The Lowdown on Key Techniques

Here‚Äôs the tea on the core methods that‚Äôve earned their stripes in research:

### 2. Retrieval Practice: Test, Don‚Äôt Just Read  
Passive reviewing is sooo last decade. Actively pulling info from memory, like quizzing yourself or using flashcards, is proven to boost test scores by up to 50% (Roediger & Karpicke, 2006). It‚Äôs basically memory gains IRL.

### 3. Interleaving: Mix It Up Like a Playlist

Pairing verbal info with visual aids (think diagrams, infographics, timelines) taps into two brain pathways, making your recall sharper (Paivio, 1991). It‚Äôs like watching the movie *and* reading the book at the same time.

### 5. Metacognition: Know Thyself, Know Thy Learning

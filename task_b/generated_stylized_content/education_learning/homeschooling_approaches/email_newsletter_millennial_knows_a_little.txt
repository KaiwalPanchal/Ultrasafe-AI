## Here are the homeschool styles you might hear about:

Think: school-at-home. Parents follow textbooks, stick to daily lessons, and test kids on core subjects like math and history. It‚Äôs the classic, no-nonsense route.

This one puts *kids in the driver‚Äôs seat*. Hands-on activities, sensory play, and learning by exploring are key. The goal? Independence with respect for each child‚Äôs pace.

### 5. Unschooling

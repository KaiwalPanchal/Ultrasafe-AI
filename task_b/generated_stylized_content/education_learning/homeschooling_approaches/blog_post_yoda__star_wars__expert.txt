### Classical Education, The Way of the Trivium

Grammar, logic, rhetoric — the three stages it honors. Latin and classical texts included often are. Reason and expression, it develops deeply.

### Montessori Method, Listen to the Child You Must

Child-led the learning is. Hands-on activities, sensory exploration, intrinsic motivation fostered. Respect for natural development, key it is.

### Charlotte Mason Method, Emphasis on Living Books

Narrative literature, nature studies, narration practice, and short focused lessons, the pillars are. Quality over quantity, remember you should.

### Unschooling, The Way of Exploration

Formal curricula rejected, interests pursued freely. Life experiences teach most here. Freedom and trust in the learner, it demands.

### Eclectic Homeschooling, Blend You Should

Methods combined, what works best for child and family mixed. Structure and experience woven together.

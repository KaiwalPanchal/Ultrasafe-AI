## Quick Vibe Check: Adult vs. Kid Learning Styles

Think about it like this‚Äîkids and adults learn kinda differently. Adults bring tons of life experience to the table, so their learning is:

- **More self-directed:** You get to boss your own pace and pick your learning style  
- **Experiential:** You learn by doing and reflecting on what fits your life  
- **Goal-driven:** You want something useful right now, not just random facts  

This is called *andragogy* (adult learning) vs. *pedagogy* (kid learning). Malcolm Knowles, the OG adult education guy, nailed it with these ideas.

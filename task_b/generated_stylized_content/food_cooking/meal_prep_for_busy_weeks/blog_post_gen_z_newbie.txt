## How to Nail Meal Prep: The Basics  

### 1. Plan Like a Boss  
Start by picking your recipes for the week ‚Äî ones that vibe with your taste, goals, and schedule. Bonus points for dishes you can cook in big batches. Also, check your pantry before buying all the groceries.

### 2. Balance the Plate  
Your meals need a little bit of everything: carbs (your energy squad), proteins (muscle builders), fats (good stuff that keeps you full), plus vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Portion control‚Äôs important too ‚Äî no overeating, please.

### 3. Keep It Safe  
Food safety is key. Most cooked meals chill fine in the fridge for up to 4 days, or freeze for 1 to 3 months (depends on the food). Use airtight, safe containers so nothing gets funky.

### 4. Batch Cook = Time Saver  
Make a big batch of stuff all at once, then split it into portions. Less cooking, more chilling.

### 5. Portion & Pack Smart  
Containers with sections or portion bags help you grab the right amount without thinking twice.

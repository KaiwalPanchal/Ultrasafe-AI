- **“Leftovers Taste Worse”**: Reheat well, season smart—you will find flavors enhanced in stews and curries, yes.  
- **“Reuse Means More Work”**: Simple planning and quick recipes save time often, reuse is not toil but aid.  
- **“Only Certain Foods Can Be Leftovers”**: Proteins, veggies, grains, even dairy—most can be repurposed safely.  
- **“Leftovers Are Unhealthy”**: Nutrients kept if safety kept; balanced diets leftovers support, they do.

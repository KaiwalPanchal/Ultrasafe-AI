### Breaking Down the Basics

**Energy Efficiency:** This means using less energy without feeling the pinch. Think tighter home seals, energy-smart appliances (hello, Energy Star!), and even smart thermostats that learn your schedule to keep you comfy and efficient.

**Net Metering:** You might get paid for extra solar power you send back to the grid. It‚Äôs like your solar system working a side hustle when you don‚Äôt need all the energy yourself.

**Battery Storage:** Lithium-ion batteries store solar energy for nighttime or power outages, so you‚Äôre covered 24/7.

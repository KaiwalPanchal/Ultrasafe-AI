## How Fast Fashion Works: The Quick Breakdown

Here‚Äôs the fast-fashion story in bite-size pieces:

3. **Transportation:** Clothing ships worldwide, using lots of energy.

## Clearing Up Some Common Confusions

It‚Äôs okay to have questions! Let‚Äôs tackle a few common myths:

- **Myth:** Fast fashion only harms people, not the planet.  
  **Truth:** The environmental harm is very real and huge, on top of social issues.

- **Myth:** Recycling clothes solves everything.  
  **Truth:** Recycling is tricky because fibers wear out and clothes mix materials, so only a little is recycled effectively.

- **Myth:** Cotton is always eco-friendly.  
  **Truth:** Regular cotton uses tons of water and chemicals, so it‚Äôs not always green.

- **Myth:** Buying lots of clothes is good for the economy and therefore okay.  
  **Truth:** The environmental and long-term costs outweigh the short-term economic boosts.

## How to Actually Save Water IRL

### At Home

- **Get low-flow fixtures:** Swap your showerheads and faucets with WaterSense-certified ones that save 20% or more water.
- **Check for leaks:** A dripping faucet can waste about 3,000 gallons a year. Fix those drips ASAP!
- **Smart watering:** Use drip irrigation and water plants early morning or late evening to avoid losing water to the sun.
- **Catch that rain:** Set up rain barrels to collect water for yards. It‚Äôs DIY and eco-friendly.

### At Work or Commercial Spots

- **Water audits:** Businesses can check exactly where water is going and sniff out leaks.
- **Optimize processes:** Industries reuse water for cooling or other steps to cut waste.
- **Train the squad:** Teach coworkers to turn taps off and be water-wise.
- **Go green:** Use permeable pavements and bioswales to soak up rainwater instead of wasting it.

## Alternatives for Common Plastic Items, Consider Them You Should

- **Plastic Bags**: Cloth or woven reusable bags, simple swaps are. Since California’s 2016 ban on single-use plastic bags, seventy-two percent reduction in litter observed (CalRecycle, 2020). Powerful, small acts are.  
- **Water Bottles**: Refillable stainless steel or glass preferred. Eighty percent reduction in single-use bottle use, observed in UK public venues with promotion of reusables (WRAP, 2019). Drink from these, you should.  
- **Straws and Cutlery**: Metal, bamboo, silicone options available. Restaurants embracing these, sixty percent decline in plastic waste experienced (Green America, 2021).  
- **Food Packaging**: Unpackaged produce or packaging of paper or biodegradable materials choose. Thirty percent store plastic waste reduction reported by supermarkets that removed plastic-wrapped fruit (Tesco, 2019).

Here are the key things to keep in mind as you plan:

## Busting Common Myths About Sustainable Travel

Thinking sustainable travel means trouble or sacrifice? Nope! Let‚Äôs clear things up:

- **Is it expensive?**  
  Not really! Sometimes green choices save you money, like taking public transport, picking local spots, or just consuming less.

- **Does skipping flights fix everything?**  
  Flights do cause a lot of emissions, but sustainability includes how you eat, where you stay, and how you manage waste, too.

- **Will I have to give up comfort?**  
  Not at all! Many eco hotels are comfy and stylish, proving you can have luxury and be green.

- **Is all nature tourism good?**  
  No, some nature tours harm animals and habitats. Always pick certified and ethical operators.

- **Can carbon offsets erase all travel impact?**  
  Offsets help, but cutting emissions first is the best way to go.

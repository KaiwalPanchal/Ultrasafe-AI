## Common Myths, Dispel Them We Must

- *Muscle loss during IF?* Short fasts and protein protect muscle well, fear not. (Tinsley et al., 2019)  
- *Eat anything while eating?* No, quality and quantity of food still matter, yes. Overeating cancels benefits.  
- *For all suitable?* No. Pregnant, with eating disorders, or certain illnesses, fasting not advised without doctor.  
- *Metabolism slows?* Short fasts raise metabolism slightly due to norepinephrine increase. (Cahill, 2006)

## Different IF Styles: Pick Your Flavor

- **16:8 (Time-Restricted Feeding):** Eat all your meals in an 8-hour window, fast the other 16. Studies show this can help with weight loss and keep metabolism humming.
- **Alternate-Day Fasting (ADF):** You eat normal one day, then fast or eat very little the next. Solid research backs this for better insulin and heart health.
- **5:2 Fasting:** Eat normally five days a week, then seriously cut calories for two days.  
- **Extended Fasts:** 24, 48, or even 72 hours (usually under medical supervision) for some hardcore cellular benefits like immune boost and brain health.

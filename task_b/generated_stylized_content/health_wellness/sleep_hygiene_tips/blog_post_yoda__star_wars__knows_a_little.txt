## Take Action, You Must: Steps To Better Sleep

### Consistent Sleep Schedule, Establish

- Go to sleep and wake daily the same time, yes—weekends too.
- If change must, gently by 15–30 minutes, do it.

### Optimize Your Sleep Place

- Keep room 18-20°C (64-68°F), a sleep haven this is.
- Blackout curtains or mask, use to banish light.
- White noise or plugs, silence unwanted sound.

### Screens and Blue Light, Limit Decisively

- One hour before bed, devices off you put.
- Blue light filters or glasses wear, if screens needed are.

### Diet and Substance, Control

- Avoid caffeine and nicotine 4 to 6 hours before sleep.
- Alcohol near bedtime, limit you must.
- Heavy meals late at night, no; light fare better.

### Gentle Pre-Sleep Rituals, Create

- Read, meditate, bathe warm—calming practices these are.
- Avoid excitement and hard exercise within last two hours.

### Exercise Regularly, Yet Wisely

- Finish intense workouts hours before sleep starts.

### Help Seek When Needed

- Persistent insomnia or sleep disorder, professional help consult.

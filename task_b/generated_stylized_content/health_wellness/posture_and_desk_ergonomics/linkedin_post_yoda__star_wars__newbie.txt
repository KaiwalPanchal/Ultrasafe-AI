- “Sit perfectly still and upright all day, you must.” False this is. Movement, rest your muscles it does.

- “Standing desks alone cure back pain.” No, they do not. Improper standing posture can cause discomfort too.

- Ergonomic tools, magic fix they are not. Education on proper use, necessary.

## A No-Equipment Home Workout You Can Try RN

**Warm-up (5-10 mins):** March in place, arm circles, some dynamic stretches to wake your body up.  
**Circuit (repeat 3x):**  
- 15 squats  
- 10 push-ups (modify with knees if needed)  
- 20 walking lunges (10 per leg)  
- 30-second plank  
- 20 jumping jacks  
**Cool down:** Chill with static stretches, focusing on muscles you worked.

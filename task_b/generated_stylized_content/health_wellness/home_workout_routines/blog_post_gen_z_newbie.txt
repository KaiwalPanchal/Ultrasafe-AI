## Simple Workout Plan You Can Start Today (No Equipment Needed)

**Warm-Up (5-10 mins):** March in place, arm circles, dynamic stretches to wake up the body  
**Circuit (Repeat 3x):**  
- 15 squats  
- 10 push-ups (knees down = totally fine!)  
- 20 walking lunges (10 per leg)  
- 30-second plank hold  
- 20 jumping jacks  
**Cool Down:** Chill with some static stretches on muscles you used

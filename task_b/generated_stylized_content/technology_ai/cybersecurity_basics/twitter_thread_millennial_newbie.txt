2/9  
cybersecurity = keeping your devices & data safe from hackers, viruses, and sneaky scams. it’s all about protecting your info so only you (and people you trust) can see it. sounds important? it is! every 8 secs there’s a global data breach (IBM, 2023). 2/9

3/9  
quick pro tip: strong passwords are your first shield! but guess what? “123456” is still the most common & *worst* password out there (NordPass, 2024). no sweat—password managers like LastPass make strong passwords a breeze! 3/9

4/9  
ever gotten a weird email asking for your details? that’s phishing—90% of hacking attempts use this trick (Verizon, 2024). don’t click sketchy links or attachments. spam filters & user smarts are your BFFs here! 4/9

5/9  
let’s talk security basics:  
- confidentiality = only you & your trusted peeps see your info  
- integrity = info stays accurate & unedited  
- availability = info & systems work when you need them  
simple, right? these are the main pillars for staying safe. 5/9

6/9  
real talk: antivirus alone won’t cut it. experts say layering security—like firewalls & multi-factor auth (MFA)—keeps you safer. MFA means logging in with 2+ steps, making it way harder for hackers to get in. 6/9

7/9  
did you know? remote work has upped cyber attacks on home Wi-Fi by 30% (Forbes, 2023). pro tip: update your router password and use WPA3 encryption. public Wi-Fi? avoid logging into important stuff there! 7/9

8/9  
big trends to watch: AI in security helps spot threats faster, while passwordless logins (think FaceID) are making life easier & safer. ransomware attacks are on the rise in 2024, so backups are your emergency plan! 8/9

9/9  
no sweat, you got this! keep your software updated, use MFA, be email-skeptical, backup your data, & learn more as you go. got questions or tips? drop ‘em below & let’s keep the cyber-baddies away together! 🚀 #StaySafeOnline 9/9

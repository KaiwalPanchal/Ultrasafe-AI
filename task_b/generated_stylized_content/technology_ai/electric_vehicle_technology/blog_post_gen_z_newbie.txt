## What‚Äôs an EV Anyway?

Alright, squad, quick rundown: Electric Vehicles (or EVs) are cars that run on electricity ‚Äî either fully or sometimes mixed with gas. Instead of sucking up gasoline like your typical car, EVs use electric motors powered by batteries.

- **Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs):** These mix both a gas engine and an electric motor. Plus, you can plug them in to recharge the battery.

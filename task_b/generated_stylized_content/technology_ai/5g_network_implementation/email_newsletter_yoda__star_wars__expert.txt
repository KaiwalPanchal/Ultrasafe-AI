**Core concepts, wise understand them you must:**

- **Network Slicing** — virtual networks many, create allowing operators. Tailored services for emergency, gaming, industrial control — diverse needs served it can.

- **Massive MIMO** — dozens, even hundreds of antennas multiplex signals they do, improving capacity and efficiency, yes.

- **Beamforming** — signals directed precisely toward users, boosting strength and taming interference.

- **Edge Computing** — data processed near users, low latency paired it is with 5G, enabling swift responses in AR and self-driving vessels.

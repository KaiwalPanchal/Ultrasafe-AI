🎒 Dreaming of seeing Europe without breaking the bank? Let’s make budget backpacking your new travel vibe!  

Backpacking through Europe means exploring tons of amazing spots while spending less—perfect for students or anyone ready to adventure without going broke. From cozy hostels to super affordable rides, Europe’s got your back.  

Here’s the lowdown:  
- **Daily budget:** Around €40–70 usually does the trick, but in budget-friendly spots like Poland or Hungary, you can vibe with just €30–50 a day.  
- **Travel smart:** Eurail passes get you on trains across 33 countries, and budget flights can be as cheap as €20. Bonus: night trains save you a night’s stay AND money!  
- **Sleep & eat:** Hostels run about €18–25 a night, and cooking your own meals at hostels with kitchens can save big bucks 🍳  

Pro tips:  
- Plan early but stay flexible to catch last-minute deals  
- Use apps like Rome2rio and Hostelworld to score the best prices  
- Pack light to dodge extra fees  
- Check out free walking tours in cities like Berlin and Prague—they’re a total steal!  

Lowkey, budget backpacking can be a huge mood if you mix planning with some spontaneous fun. Ready to level up your travel game and see more while spending less?

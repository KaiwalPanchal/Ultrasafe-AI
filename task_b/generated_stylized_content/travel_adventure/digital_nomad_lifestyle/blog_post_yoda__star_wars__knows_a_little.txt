## 8. Action Steps, Follow Them You Should

1. **Assess Work Compatibility:** Remote your work can be? Tools, communication, clients aligned must be.
2. **Plan Financially:** Travel, shelter, insurance, taxes, emergencies—budget each you must. Low-fee banks consider.
3. **Choose Destinations Wisely:** Visas, internet quality, costs, safety, and healthcare weigh carefully.
4. **Secure Reliable Technology:** Portable laptops, backups, SIM cards, mobile hotspots—must have all.
5. **Establish a Routine:** Align your hours with clients’ zones, productivity preserve.
6. **Legal and Tax Compliance:** Experts consult, visa and tax rules obey.
7. **Build Support Networks:** Online, local, join communities for strength and joy.
8. **Prioritize Well-being:** Boundaries set, exercise keep, health attended.
